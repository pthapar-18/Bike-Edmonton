WHO WE ARE

OUR VISION

Everyday cycling in Edmonton is safe, widespread, and accessible to people of all comfort levels.

OUR MISSION

Bike Edmonton is a non-profit society dedicated to making everyday cycling in Edmonton safer and more widespread by providing bicycle services, education, and civic representation. We promote the bicycle as a healthy, economical, and ecologically sound mode of transportation.

MAKE A DONATION

For over 40 years Bike Edmonton has led the charge to make Edmonton a place where cycling is not just possible but safer, easier, more fun, and more practical year round. Our work has supported funding for the Bike Plan, bike lane construction, and programs that give people resources they need to ride. We have helped thousands of people find a bike to ride and learn to fix their bike.


Your financial support makes it possible for us to keep doing this work!

DONATE A USED BIKE

You can donate your old bike to Bike Edmonton! We'll refurbish it and resell it, with all proceeds supporting our work of making cycling safe, widespread, and accessible to people of all comfort levels.


WE ALSO ACCEPT:

  • Tires
  • Usable bike parts and tools
  • Bike accessories (lights, bags, clothing, shoes, etc.)
  • Old clothing, sheets, and towels for rags
  • Consumables such as zip ties, elastic bands, degreasers, chain lube, etc.
  • Snacks for volunteers

ACKNOWLEDGEMENT OF TRADITIONAL TERRITORIES
Bike Edmonton acknowledges that we are located on Treaty 6 territory, a traditional gathering place for diverse Indigenous peoples including the Cree, Blackfoot, Métis, Nakota Sioux, Iroquois, Dene, Ojibway / Saulteaux / Anishinaabe, Inuit, and many others whose histories, languages, and cultures continue to influence our vibrant community. We acknowledge that we are all treaty people and we are committed to equity and justice for all our relations.
